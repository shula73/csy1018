Networks

Network engineer: this field is related to computer and IT Technology, it is an exiciting job to work with different professionals.

To become a network engineer you must have a knowlegde of networking because you are responsible for design, build and maintain the whole network within a company or organisation. The aim is to provide high performance from a computer network, for example network provider, this is a business and any business there is a compation, if there is reputation and good services it produces to customer, many people interested and the company get more people and it makes profit.

As a network engineer I have a very hard task especial when I build the computer network. I have to do the work precise to ensure that nothing goes wrong for example, not to lose any of company confidential data. I have to maintain an update the network with the latest technologies, for example with new hardwares, softwares and communication technologies.


Education/Qualifications

This is a great job need a special requirements, must have a degree of computer science or related but, is very crucial to have CISCO Certification of Network. CCNA(Cisco Certified Network Associate). This CNNA knowledge you have to understand and working with familiar network devices such as router, switches, hubs, bridge etc. understanding how to install, operations, confiquration and how to fix incase of troubleshooting.
